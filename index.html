About me

Human is said to be a curious being from birth. As one indulges oneself into formal education that does not encourage curiosity, the ultimate feature that make human a human fades. With a series of events in 2023, I observed it was happening with me. Learning materials for the sake of grade was never sustainable motivation.
In a quest to understand why is something the way it is, I read a book on the art of logical thinking. I found a brand now introduction to the subject of mathematics. A knowledge that the little mysteries of the world could be solved by some abstract symbols. From then on, Mathematics has been a way of making sense of the world around me. The story of linear programming being used in World War II added to the inspiration. I feel the child in me is reborn nonetheless a little older, a little wiser.
